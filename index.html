About

Data engineer with 4+ years of production experience across financial data and enterprise supply chain platforms, working primarily on Snowflake, dbt, and Azure Databricks. The work sits at the intersection of platform engineering and business outcomes, building pipelines that analysts and operations teams can rely on without needing an engineer in the loop for every question.

At Morningstar, responsible for ELT pipelines processing 40M+ daily financial records at 99%+ uptime and cut $150K in annual Snowflake compute costs through warehouse right-sizing and clustering key optimization. Has mentored junior engineers and served as technical point of contact for embedded analysts on both teams.

MS Computer Science, Wright State University. Open to onsite and hybrid roles.
